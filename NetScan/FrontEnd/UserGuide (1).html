NetScanner User Guide

This guide helps you navigate the NetScanner platform and use all its features.

Navigating NetScanner

NetScanner provides powerful network scanning capabilities. Below are key sections of the platform:

  • Home - Overview of NetScanner and its features.
  • Scan - Start Quick or Deep network scans.
  • AI Assistant - Get intelligent insights on scan results.
  • User Guide - Learn how to use NetScanner.
  • About - Information about NetScanner and its team.

Scanning Modes: Quick Scan & Deep Scan

NetScanner offers two scanning modes:

Quick Scan allows basic scans without login, while Deep Scan requires login for advanced scanning, data storage, and PDF reports.

Using Quick Scan

Quick Scan allows you to run basic scans without logging in. Follow these steps:

  • Go to the Scan page.
  • Enter the target IP address or range.
  • Select a basic scan type such as Ping Scan or Port Scan.
  • Click "Start Scan" to begin.
  • View the results instantly on the page.

Note: Quick Scan does not store scan results.

Using Deep Scan

Deep Scan provides advanced scanning options, result storage, and PDF reports.

  • Log in to your NetScanner account.
  • Navigate to the Scan page.
  • Enter the target IP, hostname, or range.
  • Choose advanced scanning options such as Service Detection or OS Detection.
  • Click "Start Deep Scan".
  • Once completed, scan results will be stored in the database.
  • Download a PDF report of the scan results.

Understanding Scan Results

After running a scan, results will appear in the Scan Results section. Deep Scan results are saved and can be accessed later.

Using AI Assistant

The AI Assistant provides insights on network vulnerabilities, suggests security improvements, and helps interpret scan results.

Managing Your Account

Deep Scan requires an account. Login to access stored results, download reports, and configure scan preferences.
